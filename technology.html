Experience NAKED’S DANDELION PROJECT in Kyoto: A Journey of Art and Peace

NAKED’S DANDELION PROJECT has made its mark at seven iconic locations throughout Kyoto, including the famous Kiyomizu-dera and Heian Shrine. This summer, from July 3 to August 5, the immersive art installation will be showcased at Kosho-ji Temple, offering a unique nighttime experience.

DISCOVER KOSHO-JI TEMPLE

Kosho-ji Temple, renowned for its stunning autumn foliage and designated as a scenic beauty spot by Kyoto prefecture, is the perfect host for this captivating event. Visitors can wander along Kotozaka, a gently sloping path that runs from the front gate along the Uji River to the temple entrance. The path is named for the koto-like sound produced by the flowing water, adding a serene auditory backdrop to the visual spectacle.

ILLUMINATED NIGHTS AND CULTURAL PATHWAYS

During the event, the temple grounds and gardens will be beautifully illuminated, creating a magical evening ambiance. The Uji Light Path will further enhance the experience by lining the town’s streets with lanterns inspired by The Tale of Genji, adding a touch of historical elegance.

INTERACTIVE ART FOR PEACE

Attendees will have the opportunity to engage with NAKED's popular interactive features, including the NAKED Distanced Lanterns and NAKED Flower Cleansing. These activities not only provide entertainment but also invite participants to reflect on themes of peace and unity

A GLOBAL ART MOVEMENT

Since its inception in the fall of 2020, the DANDELION PROJECT has spread its message of peace at various prestigious venues, such as Tokyo Tower in Tokyo and Kosho-ji Temple in Kyoto. The project envisions "a world full of peace," using network-type art to connect people worldwide. Dandelion art objects are planted globally, and participants who interact with these installations send digital flowers as symbols of their peace wishes. These digital dandelion fluffs are transmitted across the network, allowing flower art to bloom worldwide.

Join the Experience

Visitors can participate in this unique art piece by holding up their smartphones to witness the digital dandelion fluff dance and disperse. This interactive experience symbolizes the spread of peace and harmony, inviting everyone to be part of a global artistic endeavor. Don’t miss this enchanting blend of art, culture, and technology at Kosho-ji Temple this summer. Engage with NAKED’S DANDELION PROJECT and be part of a global movement for peace.

Stay at Henn Na Hotel: Where Dinosaurs Greet You

Why not experience a hotel stay where dinosaurs greet you at the front desk? Welcome to "Henn na Hotel," a state-of-the-art establishment that combines comfort and enjoyment with cuttingedge technology. This innovative hotel is certified by Guinness World Records as the first hotel staffed by working robots, offering a truly unique and luxurious stay.

A FUTURISTIC WELCOME

Upon arrival, guests are welcomed by two uniformed robot receptionists who guide them through the check-in process. These multilingual robots can converse in English, Korean, and Chinese, ensuring a smooth and friendly greeting for international visitors. Additionally, a robot is available to handle your luggage, transporting it to your room, which can be accessed via facial recognition technology

INNOVATIVE IN-ROOM TECHNOLOGY

Each room at Henn na Hotel is equipped with Tapia, a voice-recognition robot that controls the lights, provides the time, and announces the weather. Guests also have access to 'Handy,' a smartphone that serves as a remote control for the television and air conditioning, and offers free national and international calls. For added convenience, the LG Styler in every room cleans and irons clothes using spray and vibration technology

CONCIERGE SERVICES BY UNIBO

Unibo, the friendly robot concierge, is available to chat with guests and provide information about local attractions, shops, and restaurants. This interactive experience ensures you have all the information you need to make the most of your stay

SMART AMENITIES

Rooms also feature a Smart Plate that displays tourist information and offers games, enhancing both entertainment and convenience during your stay.

UNIQUE ROBOT EXPERIENCES

The types of robots and their functionalities vary across different Henn na Hotel locations, adding an element of surprise and variety to each visit.
